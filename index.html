Here are answers to some common questions about business registration, compliance, and SME support with TASGOC Consultants.

TASGOC Consultants specializes in company registration, compliance management, SME certification, and financial advisory services tailored to help Namibian businesses grow legally and sustainably.

Registration timelines vary depending on the business type, but TASGOC strives to streamline the process to minimize delays, often completing registrations within days.

Businesses must comply with tax filings (Income Tax, VAT, PAYE), Social Security Commission registration, and relevant industry licenses. TASGOC provides ongoing support to ensure compliance.

TASGOC assists SMEs with certification applications for government tenders, business plan development, financial record-keeping, and wage return filings to empower sustainable growth.
